# Android Phone Automation Tool

## Overview

MadHub's powerful automation features enable you to streamline the management of your Android phone tasks. This Android phone automation tool provides a comprehensive suite of capabilities to optimize your social media management experience, ensuring that your accounts remain active and engaging without the constant manual effort.

## Key Features

### 1. Facebook Account Warm-Up
MadHub's **Facebook Account Warm-Up** feature simulates authentic user behavior on your Facebook account. This includes browsing homepage feeds and video pages, intelligently skipping ad videos, and performing like and comment operations. With customizable settings, you can adjust the interaction and execution probabilities to fine-tune how your account engages with content.

#### Capabilities:
- Enhances account activity and trustworthiness
- Reduces the risk of account bans
- Ideal for nurturing new accounts or maintaining old ones

#### Usage:
To use the **Facebook Account Warm-Up**, simply configure the interaction settings in MadHub's graphical user interface. You can select the frequency of actions, the types of posts to interact with, and control the randomness of the interactions.

#### Configuration Examples:
- **Set Interaction Probability:** Adjust the likelihood of liking or commenting on posts.
- **Execution Probability Distribution:** Customize how often certain types of interactions occur, allowing for a more natural user behavior simulation.

### 2. Facebook Group Auto-Posting
Another powerful feature of MadHub is the **Facebook Group Auto-Posting**. This feature allows you to automate the posting process across multiple Facebook groups that you are a member of, maximizing your outreach without the hassle of manual posting.

#### Capabilities:
- Supports both input mode (text posts) and file mode (image/video posts)
- Allows for content rotation and scheduling of posts
- Flexible control over the posting frequency and target groups

#### Usage:
To utilize the **Facebook Group Auto-Posting** feature, specify the groups you wish to target and the content to be posted. You can also set the total number of posts per group and schedule intervals for automated posting.

#### Configuration Examples:
- **Single Group Post Count:** Handle how many posts are made to one group at a time.
- **Total Post Count:** Set the overall number of posts to be made across all selected groups.
- **Insert Images:** Easily add images to your posts to enhance engagement.

### 3. TikTok Account Warm-Up
MadHub's **TikTok Account Warm-Up** feature caters specifically to TikTok users aiming to grow their accounts organically. This function allows for a seamless simulation of user behavior tailored for TikTok, enhancing account credibility and reducing the chance of detection as a bot.

#### Capabilities:
- Supports both vertical nurturing (keyword-focused browsing) and random content discovery
- Engages with videos through likes and comments based on configurable probabilities
- Establishes trust and activity for both new and mature TikTok accounts

#### Usage:
Activate the **TikTok Account Warm-Up** feature by selecting your preferred browsing mode. You can tailor which types of videos your account interacts with, ensuring content relevance and interest alignment.

#### Configuration Examples:
- **Set Interaction Probability:** Define how likely your account is to engage with videos.
- **Execution Probability Distribution:** Control how diverse your interactions are across the platform.
